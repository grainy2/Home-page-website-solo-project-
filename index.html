Visit Kaduna

Enjoy local festivals, rich heritage and vibrant sports in northern Nigeria.

Top three activities to do at Kaduna

Attend a show or a concert

Murtala Square is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a piknic in the local park

Centenary park is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the kaduna stadium

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
